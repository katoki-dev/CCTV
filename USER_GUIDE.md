## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

1. Open your browser and navigate to `http://localhost:5000`
2. Login with your credentials (Default: `admin` / `admin`)
3. You'll be directed to the **Dashboard**

---

## Using the AI Chatbot

The CEMSS chatbot is your personal security assistant, available 24/7 to help you monitor and manage your surveillance system.

### Opening the Chatbot

- Click the **chat icon** in the bottom-right corner of any page
- The chatbot window will slide open

### What Can the Chatbot Do?

#### 📹 **Visual Queries**

Ask the chatbot to analyze camera feeds in real-time:

- **"What do you see on camera 1?"** - Get detailed scene description
- **"Show me camera 2"** - Analyze specific camera
- **"What's happening?"** - Analyze default camera

#### 📊 **System Status**

Get information about your surveillance system:

- **"How many cameras are active?"** - Check system status
- **"Show me recent detections"** - View latest events
- **"Show alerts"** - Check alert history

#### 🔍 **Detection Queries**

Search and filter detection logs:

- **"Show detections from the last hour"**
- **"Any fall detections today?"**
- **"Show me person detections"**

#### 💬 **General Questions**

The chatbot can answer general questions too:

- **"Hello!"** - Get a friendly greeting
- **"How can you help me?"** - Learn about capabilities
- **"What's the capital of France?"** - General knowledge

### 🎯 **Pro Tips**

1. **Be Specific**: Mention camera numbers for targeted analysis
2. **Natural Language**: Talk normally - the AI understands context
3. **Follow-up Questions**: The chatbot remembers your conversation
4. **Time Frames**: Specify "today", "last hour", "this week" for filtered results

## Troubleshooting

### Chatbot Not Responding

- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Ensure Ollama service is running

### Camera Not Loading

- Verify camera is connected
- Check camera status in Admin panel
- Restart the camera feed

### Need Help?

Contact your system administrator or consult the [Admin Guide](ADMIN_GUIDE.md)

## Security Best Practices

1. **Change Default Password**: Update admin credentials immediately
2. **Regular Backups**: Back up detection logs periodically
3. **Monitor Alerts**: Review alerts daily
4. **Camera Maintenance**: Keep camera lenses clean
